PWP Milestones 1: "Elevated Chai"


Purpose, Audience & Goal

PURPOSE: Provide information about an all organic, delicious chai mix that boosts health and gives sustained energy without crash.

AUDIENCE: Health conscious consumers wanting an alternative drink to coffee, that is rich and delicious. People that are sensitive to coffee but want a really good tasting ritual drink and don't mind spending a little bit more time to prepare in order to get maximum health benefits and best taste. The audience would be people that might shop at a natural food stores (ie: wholefoods) and use alternative medicine.

GOAL: Landing page to increase interest and inform users about the Chai product that is coming to market and the benefits. Provide details on the health benefits of the tea and why it is different than typical chai-tea. Collect emails for users who wish to be notified about the product and when it is available or to sign up to receive a sample.


Persona

NAME: Maya

AGE: 30

PROFESSION: Yoga Instructor

TECHNOLOGY: Has a desktop computer at home, but mostly uses her iPhone to listen to music, post and browse on social media apps, and order things online. She is fairly comfortable using technology for basic things, like searching, ordering online, and making simple digital flyers using tools like canva. She doesn't like being on tech a lot, but knows it's a necessary tool to market to people her classes.

ATTITUDES AND BEHAVIOURS: Values health and wellness as a top priority. Seeks out natural and organic products and willing to pay extra for high quality ingredients. The environment and sustainability is important, so she values products that have eco packaging that doesnt generate more waste. She is willing to spend time to prepare healthy fresh food and drink, as apposed to quick/fast food that might be unhealthy. Values time to write in her journal, think, read while enjoying a rich hot or cold drink. As a busy Yoga teacher she uses social media to connect and interact with her students, posting about upcoming events and photos from the yoga studio.

FRUSTRATIONS AND NEEDS: Maya loves the ritual of having a delicious cup of coffee in the morning, but feels wired from the caffeine and hates the crash later in the day. She wants to have a hot drink that is healthy and gives her some natural energy without the ill effects of drinking coffee. Teaching her students requires a lot of focused energy output and she needs to stay healthy and alert to be able to do what she does. When she gets sick it creates a lot more stress for her because she has to cancel and reschedule classes and loses income from not working. She wants a drink that also has other health benefits like immune boosting properties so she can stay healthy. Other healthy drinks she has tried like MUD\WTR doesn't taste very good and lacks the cozy and rich experience she likes with drinking a good cup of coffee at home or in a cafe.

GOALS: Find a drink that is delicious, comforting, and gives her energy and immune system boost. She wants to find a high quality product online that is rated highly so she will feel confident that it is a good product.

USER STORY: As a health-conscious yoga instructor, I want to discover an all organic, delicious chai mix that gives me sustained energy without the negative effects of coffee and so that I can stay focused and support my wellness.
